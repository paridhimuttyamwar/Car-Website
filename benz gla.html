Mercedes-Benz GLA

Exterior:

The new GLA impresses at first glance with its improved front end design with diamond radiator grille with chrome pins, while the light-alloy wheels embody unique driving comfort. Single louvre in matt grey with insert in chrome and accents in high-gloss black at the front grille. Dynamic elegance radiates from the new rear end design with distinctive tail pipes and fully-LED rear lights. The new paint finish in the extravagant spectral blue completes the fine design .

Interior :

The Mercedes-Benz GLA interior impresses with its high-quality finish, generous sense of space and innovative driving comfort. The steering wheel, the redesigned centre console and the stylish ambient lighting. The new MBUX multimedia system adapts to your wishes, bringing you a one-of-a-kind intuitive in-car experience.

Colors Available :

Mercedes-Benz GLA is available in 4 different colours -Mountain Grey, Iridium Silver, Polar White and Cosmos Black.

Mileage :

The mileage of Mercedes-Benz GLA top model (GLA 220d 4MATIC AMG Line) is up to 18.9 kmpl.

Mercedes-Benz S-class

Exterior :

The exterior of the S-Class have a Superior look and a luxurious feeling of spaciousness in ideal harmony: the design idiom of sensuous purity in its most modern form.

Interior :

The interior of the S-Class combines a broad selection of elegant materials with fine surfaces. Innovative technology and high-resolution screens lend it a progressive character. Furthermore, high-tech and infotainment are presented with a user-friendly architecture.

Colors Available :

There are 5 colors available for S-Class - Selenite Grey, Designo Diamond White Bright, High Tech Silver, Onyx Black and Graphite Grey.

Mileage :

The average mileage of Mercedes-Benz S-Class as tested/ as claimed by the manufacturer is 18 kmpl.The fuel tank of Mercedes-Benz S-Class has an official capacity of 76.0 litres.

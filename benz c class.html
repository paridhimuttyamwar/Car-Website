Mercedes-Benz C-class

Variants :

The C-Class is offered in 3 variants namely C 300, C 200, C 220d. The cheapest Mercedes-Benz C-Class variant is the C 200 which has a price tag of ₹ 61.85 Lakh while the most expensive variant is the Mercedes-Benz C-Class C 300 which commands a price of ₹ 69 Lakh.

Exterior :

The exterior design of the Mercedes-Benz C-Class impresses with its progressive appearance. The design idiom of the Mercedes-Benz C-Class was systematically thought through down to the last detail.

Colors Available :

Mercedes-Benz C-Class is available in 5 different colours - Mojave Silver Metallic, High Tech Silver, Obsidian Black Metallic, Selenite Grey Metallic and Sodalite Blue Metallic.

Mileage :

The mileage of Mercedes-Benz C-Class top model (C-Class C 300) is up to 15 kmpl.The fuel tank of Mercedes-Benz C-Class has an official capacity of 66.0 litres.

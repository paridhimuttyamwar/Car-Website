The split taillights impress with their new contour and an unmistakable star-look interior. Interior of the E-Class sets new standards not just in terms of design. Mercedes-Benz E-Class is available in 5 different colours - High Tech Silver, Graphite Grey, Obsidian, Polar White and Nautic Blue.

The average mileage of Mercedes-Benz E-Class as tested/ as claimed by the manufacturer is 15 kmpl. The fuel tank of Mercedes-Benz E-Class has an official capacity of 66.0 litres.
